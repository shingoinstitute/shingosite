Corporate Overview

PyMPSA is a company within PiSA Group, LEADER in the pharmaceutical industry, engaged in the development and manufacturing of components and high quality medical equipment with over 25 years of experience in Mexico and throughout the world.

PiSA GROUP is a 100% Mexican pharmaceutical company that emerged in 1945. PiSA Pharmaceutical has 7 manufacturing plants with over 14,000 employees allowing us to offer more than 1,000 pharmaceutical products and 3 affiliated plants to manufacture medical devices, veterinary products, gauze and surgical drapes.

Currently, the list of our major clients include international companies such as Baxter, Bayer and Carefusion, as well as Mexican government institutes such as IMSS (Mexican Institute of Social Security) that gives medical attention to over 59 million affiliates and the ISSSTE (Institute of Security and Social Services for State Workers) which gives medical attention to more than 13 million Mexicans.

PyMPSA currently has presence in over 20 countries in Central America, South America, The Caribbean, Europe, Middle East, and also in United States, through the exportation of own products, manufacturing of products to third parties with own brand and “tailor made” products (OEM Original Equipment Manufacturer) for various companies.

We have more than 5.93 acres of modern and spacious facilities including 8 clean rooms for the manufacture of products class 10,000 and 100,000 with all the services and controls required such as air filtration, positive pressure, temperature and humidity control, strict access procedures, cleaning and sanitization, all under a monitoring and control system.

Our Procedures

PyMPSA has automatic machinery and equipment with the newest technology for the processes of extrusion, injection, stamping, assembly, packaging, sterilization, measurement and testing of products; all that in addition to the design and development of our products.

Mission

We are a manufacturing company of medical devices, focused on fulfilling with discipline the expectations of customers through quality and timely delivery of our products, supporting our operation with certified staff, cutting edge technology and an agile and effective administration, achieving the expected profitability of the company.

Vision

"Being recognized as the world class leader manufacturing company of medical devices in the Americas.”

Our People:

PyMPSA has over 1,300 professionals across 8 production chains committed to excellence and aware that each of their actions work in favor of life.

Our Products

PyMPSA has a wide range of products that include: infusion equipment, Burette equipment, 3- way stopcock Extension, urine bags, parenteral and enteral nutrition bags, peritoneal dialysis bags, and Hemoline epidural equipment.

Experience with Continuous Improvement

Since 2009 PyMPSA has incorporated the working philosophy "Lean Six Sigma," developing improvement techniques such as Hoshin Kanri, VSM, Kaizen Teian, Kaizen, TPM and Six Sigma Improvement Projects.

Achievements 2013

  • Quality: Average of 4.5 PPM's
  • Cost: 80% improvement in revenue per peroson during the last 4 years
  • Delivery: 95% of our orders delivered on time; 26 new products launched in 2013

Morale

  • Record of 1,268,080 hours free of disabling accidents
  • Reduction of ergonomic problems with the automation of our processes
  • Current staff turnover of 0.5% per month

Social Responsibility

PiSA demonstrates its commitment to the community through the Stella Vega Foundation named after the late wife of PiSA’s owner. Through the Stella Vega foundation the community has been supported during the 1st half of 2013 in the following programs:

Health: 2,645 hemodialysis sessions, 1,339 medicines delivered, 68 patients benefited from clinical studies.

Disasters: Support in natural disasters as in the case of Hurricanes Ingrid and Manuel and support throughout the ecocide occurred in the Dam of Valencia in Jalisco.

Food: 10,832 Pantries delivered to different institutions and 500 Pantries delivered to low- income families.

Donations: More than 66 different institutions have received donations from PiSA and 19,332 dialysis bags given to patients with kidney problems.

Social Responsibility:

  • 50 Employees volunteered to accompany patients on hemodialysis treatment in SANEFRO (Nephrology Sanatoriums).
  • Special support is given to patients with very limited resources transporting them to their hemodialysis sessions.
