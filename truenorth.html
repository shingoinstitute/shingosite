True North-Compass, LLC

http://www.truenorth-compass.com/

True North-Compass (TNC) is an evidence-based consulting firm located in the Twin Cities area of Minnesota, USA. TNC’s mission is to assist small to midsize organizations (100-1,000 employees) with its continuous improvement, leadership development and organizational development efforts. TNC’s goal is to help clients compete globally by providing the best value to its customers through culture alignment, operational excellence and employee engagement. TNC strives to approach efforts with its clients holistically to create sustainable results by collaborative use of correct principles, systems and tools.

TNC’s expertise is wide-ranged. Through a talented and diverse staff, it frequently helps clients in the following domains: lean and six sigma deployment, leadership and organizational development, customized training and business process improvement.
